About AutoWash

AutoWash is an innovative solution designed to automate the process of hand washing. Our system ensures optimal water usage, efficient hand washing, and hygiene.

Our Mission

To provide an automated, efficient, and hygienic hand washing experience that conserves water and promotes health and safety.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of automated hand washing systems globally, ensuring clean hands for everyone, everywhere.
